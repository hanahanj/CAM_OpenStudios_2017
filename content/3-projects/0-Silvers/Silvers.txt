Dexter's Silvers is widely known for his unique oil on canvas depictions of some of St Louis' most popular landmarks, as well as portraits, and drawings. He is self taught and enjoys teaching others and sharing his gift with the world. Dexter's Art Studio also offers private paint parties and monthly exhibitions.
