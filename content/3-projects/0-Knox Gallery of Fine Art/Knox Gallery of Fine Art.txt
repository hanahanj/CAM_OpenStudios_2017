Knox Gallery of Fine Art features original works by a variety of artists. Here the discerning collector will find that unique piece just perfect for the spot waiting in his or her home or office. Interior designers will be able to generate room settings with one-of-a-kind items from artists whose creativity is equal to their own. Isn’t that the goal of every designer? Did you ever receive a boring gift? One that someone gave just because they “had to”? No one will say that about gifts purchased from Knox Gallery. And there is a fit for any budget! Come visit frequently to see what is new in the world of St Louis art.
