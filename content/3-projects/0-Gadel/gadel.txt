###**Media Type:**
###Craft/Traditional Arts, Painting, Printmaking, Ceramics



My works in clay reflect images that carry a lot of meaning for me. I try to capture them permanently in a slightly abstract sense. My paintings, at the moment, are all about the patterns and colors. I love to work with the paint, applying it in small dots that become larger patterns.
