Coulter Photography and Films is a Saint Louis based company which specializes in documentary, architectural, nature, and portrait photography in addition to independent film production. As well Coulter Photography and Films have collaborated with the RAMS organization and individual NFL athletes to provide one-of-a- kind artistically created athletic gear, individual player photographs, and documentary film with a distinctive artistic style for the past three years.
