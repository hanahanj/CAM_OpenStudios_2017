###**Media Type:**
###Ceramics, Fiber, Mixed Media



Hannah Montford combines reclaimed materials with painting, printmaking, drawing, collage, fibers and ceramics to tell stories of ephemeral memories, fleeting imprints, human relationships and the ethereal world we inhabit. She draws the viewer into her work using repetition, geometric pattern, organic form, surface texture and vibrant color fields. Hannah Montford holds a BFA in Painting and Graphic Design and a Masters in Aesthetic Education from Webster University. She is a high school ceramics teacher in the Ferguson-Florissant School District.
