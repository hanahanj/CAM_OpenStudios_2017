####The St. Louis Artists' Guild
###12 Jackson Ave
###St. Louis, MO 63105

###_Studio Open Saturday, October 7_

###**Media Type:**
###Fiber, Printmaking, Painting



Members, instructors and students have access to our large classroom/studio that is equipped for painting, drawing, printmaking, fiber arts and mixed-media.
The following artists will have artwork on display:
Suzy Farren-Fiber Art 
Morris Fletcher-Painting
Briana Kagy-Printmaking
Sandra Kolde-Fiber Art
Ruth Kolker-Printmaking
Erica Popp-Printmaking
Bryan Robertson-Painting
Joanne Stremsterfer-Printmaking
Timothy Wagner-Multi-media
Ping Yan-Chinese brush painting
Maggie Zografakis-Mixed-media painting

Schedule:
11:00-Wood carving demonstration with Briana Kagy
12-2-Fun FREE First Saturday art project led by Timothy Wagner
2:00-Printmaking demonstration with Erica Popp
3:00-Chinese brush painting workshop with Ping Yan.



**Studio Features:**

- Is Child-Friendly
- Will Have Food
- Will Have Drinks
- Will Include Work For Sale
- Is Participatory/Interactive
- Handicapped-accessible
