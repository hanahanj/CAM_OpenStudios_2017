###Architecture/Urban Design, Ceramics, Digital Media, Drawing, Graphic Design/Illustration, Installation, Mixed Media, Painting, Photography, Sculpture, Social Practice/Community Art or Engagement



Phi Academy of Art offers students of all ages and levels a classical education in drawing, painting, and sculpture. Phi Academy of Art was founded by artist and sculptor Phil Perschbacher. Phi Academy offers twice weekly long pose art groups as well as individual and group classes. Students can begin at any time. Learn from master teachers traditional art skills to help develop and refine your craft.



**Studio Features:**

- Is Child-Friendly
