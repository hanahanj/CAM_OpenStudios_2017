###**Media Type:**
###Drawing, Fiber, Jewelry, Printmaking, Mixed Media, Painting



Garage Studios is a collaboration featuring Marnie Claunch, Marina Claunch, Brian Barkhurst, Katherine Avant, Beverly Brandt, Karen Bult, Heidi Morgan, Cindy Pastard, Charlotte Schweppe, CJ Mitchell, Liam Cassidy, Shawna Williams, Jennifer Flynn, and Adelia Parker-Castro. This multi-artist group includes printmakers, painters, crafters, and mixed media artists that use a variety of techniques and subject matter. Garage Studios regularly hosts meet-ups where individuals engage in various playful art making processes. They will also have an assortment of art for sale including art prints, cards, jewelry, and paintings.
