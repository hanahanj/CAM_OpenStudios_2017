###**Media Type:**
###Jewelry, Sculpture



Using a small propane and oxygen torch artist melts and forms glass into beads and sculptures for jewelry and decorative items. Using colorful glass rods artist forms beads and sculptures on steel rods to create small works of art that can then be assembled into various unique jewelry pieces.
