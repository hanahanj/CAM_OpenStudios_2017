###_Studio Open Saturday, October 7_

###**Media Type:**
###Paintings, Ceramics, etc.



This exhibit and open house features the work of St Louis-based artist educators. In collaboration with the public and as a form of community engagement - all sales of stemming from featured artwork benefits St Louis children, the mission of the Colorbridge Arts Collective and dedicated art educators.



**Studio Features:**

- Is Child-Friendly
- Participatory/Interactive
- Will Have Food
- Will Have Drinks
- Will Include Work For Sale
- Handicapped-accessible
