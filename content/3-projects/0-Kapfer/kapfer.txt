###**Media Type:**
###Painting



Jeff Kapfer earned a BFA from Webster University with an emphasis in graphic design. He combines his graphic design influence with his childhood obsession with birds to create vibrant light-hearted paintings inspired by friends, family and everyday life. His work has been shown throughout St Louis including Houska Gallery, SOHA Gallery, and SIBA Gallery.
