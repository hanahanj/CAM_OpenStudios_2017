###**Media Type:**
###Digital Media, Graphic Design/Illustration, Photography



Robin Lewis-Heagler specializes in beauty/glamour and fashion photography. 

She is also known for her lifestyle photography work and is a sought after High School Senior portrait artist in the Metro St. Louis area. Robin is also the founder and publisher of Visionary Magazine which is a print and online publication catering to the visual arts and features some of the best artists from all over the world.
